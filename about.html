Rayan Saffron Co.

Rayan Herat Saffron, a private company, stands out as one of the leading and largest manufacturers and exporters of Afghan Saffron. Specializing in the processing, packaging, trading, and global exportation of high-quality and premium saffron, the company maintains stringent quality control measures. From harvesting raw materials to cleaning, sorting, packing, metal detection, vacuum fumigation, inspection, and container loading, all activities are closely monitored by international quality control organizations.In addition to its reputation for offering excellent and premium saffron, the company is renowned for its prompt delivery services to customers worldwide.


Rayan Herat Saffron is located in Injil District of Herat province and offers and sells the highest grades of diverse saffron types sourced from Afghanistan, celebrated for their purity, aroma, and taste, recognized both locally and globally. To ensure customer satisfaction, the company maintains enduring and long-standing relationships with saffron farmers who supply them with valuable and pure Afghan saffron.

The company began its business operations in 2019 in Herat, Afghanistan, focusing on the processing, packaging, and trading of saffron. The company's team consists of professional experts and well-experienced staff dedicated to providing high-quality and cost-effective saffron to satisfy both national and international customers.

It has been three years since the company was registered in the United States of America, and it has been awarded an FDA certificate with REG #: 13286924580. It’s worth mentioning that the company's saffron products have reached many states in America and have passed FDA laboratory tests, which reassure their customers about the aroma, purity, color strength (crocin), and high quality of their saffron products.
Weston Trade Inc., the company’s international US FDA partner based in Los Angeles, CA, sells its saffron products in small packages of 1 gm, 2 gm, and 4 gm under the brand names Sahar and Khorasan in US markets. Moreover, the company's saffron products are available in many food stores and supermarkets in the US.

The company's saffron enjoys a strong reputation among international buyers for several reasons:

  • Exceptional taste, aroma, and superior quality and color.
  • Guaranteed 100% natural and pure.
  • Grade A quality offered at reasonable prices.
  • Sourced from the best regions in the country during harvest.
  • Available in packaging sizes ranging from 0.5 gm to 1000 gm.
  • Using equipped and scientific laboratory, modern and beautiful packing line, careful quality control eyes, fast and effective logistic.
  • Export possible to all the worldwide.

Company Strategy/Purpose:

To enhance and expand the saffron market through dedicated expertise, the company produces, processes, and packages according to international standards, enabling it to offer Afghan saffron to global markets at competitive prices.

Company Mission:

To consistently enhance its global reputation as a leader in saffron cultivation, harvesting, processing, and trading for the international market, as well as in saffron product consulting. The company aims to pioneer quality solutions, delivering excellent professional services regardless of project size or profitability.

Company Vision:

Rayan Herat Saffron is dedicated to enhancing its professional services continually and seeks to create opportunities for highly skilled professionals to contribute. The company leverages its expertise to serve society's development with the utmost professionalism, integrity, and dedication.

Mohammad Fahim Mayar

Mohammad Fahim Mayar is a BSC graduate from Kabul University, known for his dynamic roles in technology and community activism. Currently serving as the Marketing and Creative Manager at Rayan Saffron, leading Saffron Production Co in Afghanistan, Fahim also holds key positions as the YouthIGFA Co-Coordinator, member of the Internet Society Chapter of Afghanistan, Executive member at AfSIG, and member of NITPAA. With a passion for digital inclusion and STEM education, Fahim advocates for the advancement of these initiatives in Afghanistan. Beyond his professional endeavors, he is a blogger, social activist, and human rights defender. In his free time, Fahim enjoys reading, biking, cooking, and nurturing his spiritual growth. Always eager to learn, Fahim embodies a spirit of continual growth and exploration.
